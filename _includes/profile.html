Chengxi Zhang 张承玺


PhD, Associate Professor
I was born in Qufu, China. I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT, 哈尔滨工业大学), China, in 2012 and 2015, and the Ph.D. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (上海交通大学), China. I worked at HIT, China from 2020-2022.
Since 2022, I have been an Associate Professor of Jiangnan Univerisity, Wuxi China.
