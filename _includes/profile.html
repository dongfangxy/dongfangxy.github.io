Chengxi Zhang 张承玺

PhD · Associate Professor · 副教授、博士生导师

I was born in Qufu, China. I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT, 哈尔滨工业大学), China, in 2012 and 2015, and the Ph.D. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (上海交通大学), China. I worked at HIT, China, from 2020 to 2022 and received the Outstanding Postdoc Awards of HIT Shenzhen in 2022. Since 2022, I have been an Associate Professor at Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China.
