I was born in Qufu, Shandong, China. I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in electronic science and technology from the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT, 哈尔滨工业大学), China, in 2012 and 2015, and the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU, 上海交通大学), China, in 2019. I worked at HIT Shenzhen, China, from 2020-2022, as Post-Doc and received the [Outstanding Postdoctoral Awards of HIT Shenzhen](http://www.hitsz.edu.cn/article/view/id-118575.html). Since 2022, I am an Associate Professor of [Jiangnan Univerisity, Wuxi China (江南大学, 中国无锡)](https://www.jiangnan.edu.cn/).
